About

My Story

I’m a UI/UX designer with a background in tourism and event management, where I gained a lot of knowledge about human behavior, communicating with all kinds of customers and truly understanding their problems.

Incorporating both my experience in human-centered work and my endless joy to be creative, I want to build products that are truly delightful to use and visually appealing.

In my free time, you can find me chasing waves, lost in nature, connecting with others, doing sports, learning, reading or practicing yoga - basically engaging with my surroundings as much as possible.
